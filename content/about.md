### Howdy!
My name is Kevin and I'm the founder of [Squid Alerts](https://www.squidalerts.com), an on-call and incident management tool.

I write the Squid Alerts blog to provide transparency into what we're building, why we're building it, and the struggles along the way. Aside from the typical product news, this is an engineering blog. There will be code, architecture diagrams, and when necessary, postmortems.

Sometimes we make the right decisions and other times... not so much. Working in public will hopefully increase the ratio of right vs wrong decisions.

### Blog Technology
If you're curious (and hopefully you are if you've found your way here). This blog is built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io) and makes use of the [Pure Theme](https://github.com/xiaoheiAh/hugo-theme-pure). Hugo is a static site generator built in Go. Since performance is a feature, we're making more and more use of static pages across our product.
